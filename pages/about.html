About Our Clinic

Learn about our mission, team, and approach to physiotherapy

Our Story

Active Life Physiotherapy Clinic was founded in 2022 with a simple mission: to provide exceptional physiotherapy care that helps people recover faster and live better.

What started as a small practice has grown into a trusted healthcare provider in the community, known for personalized treatment plans and effective results.

Our approach combines evidence-based techniques with compassionate care, ensuring that each patient receives the attention and treatment they deserve.

Our Mission & Values

Patient-Centered Care

We put our patients first, designing personalized treatment plans that address individual needs and goals.

Excellence in Treatment

We are committed to providing the highest quality care using evidence-based techniques and modern equipment.

Continuous Learning

Our team regularly updates their knowledge and skills to provide the most effective treatments available.

Meet Our Team

Dr. Maniska Yadav

Founder & Chief Physiotherapist

Dr. Yadav has 3 years of experience in physiotherapy with specialization in orthopedic rehabilitation and sports injuries. She completed her Doctorate in Physiotherapy from Delhi University.

Her approach focuses on identifying the root cause of pain and dysfunction, rather than just treating symptoms.
