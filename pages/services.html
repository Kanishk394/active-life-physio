Our Services

Comprehensive physiotherapy solutions for your health and wellness

How We Can Help You

At Active Life Physiotherapy Clinic, we offer a wide range of specialized physiotherapy services to address various conditions and help you achieve optimal health and mobility.

Our team of experienced physiotherapists uses evidence-based techniques and modern equipment to provide personalized treatment plans tailored to your specific needs.

Orthopedic Rehabilitation

Our orthopedic rehabilitation program focuses on restoring function and mobility after injuries, surgeries, or conditions affecting the musculoskeletal system.

We treat:
  • Joint pain and arthritis
  • Post-surgical rehabilitation
  • Fracture recovery
  • Tendon and ligament injuries
  • Back and neck pain
Book for Orthopedic Rehab

Sports Injury Treatment

Our sports physiotherapy services are designed to help athletes of all levels recover from injuries, improve performance, and prevent future problems.

We specialize in:
  • Acute sports injuries
  • Rehabilitation for return to sport
  • Performance enhancement
  • Injury prevention strategies
  • Sports-specific conditioning
Book for Sports Injury

Neurological Rehabilitation

Our neurological rehabilitation program helps patients recovering from neurological conditions improve their function, mobility, and quality of life.

We work with patients experiencing:
  • Stroke recovery
  • Parkinson's disease
  • Multiple sclerosis
  • Balance disorders
  • Peripheral neuropathy
Book for Neuro Rehab

Pain Management

Our comprehensive pain management approach combines various techniques to reduce pain, improve function, and enhance quality of life for those suffering from chronic or acute pain.

Our techniques include:
  • Manual therapy
  • Therapeutic exercises
  • Electrotherapy
  • Heat and cold therapy
Book for Pain Management

Women's Health Physiotherapy

Our women's health physiotherapy services address the unique physical health concerns that women may experience throughout different life stages.

We provide care for:
  • Prenatal and postnatal care
  • Pelvic floor dysfunction
  • Incontinence issues
  • Osteoporosis management
  • Pregnancy-related pain
Book for Women's Health

Geriatric Physiotherapy

Our geriatric physiotherapy services are designed to address the unique needs of older adults, helping them maintain independence and improve quality of life.

We focus on:
  • Balance and fall prevention
  • Mobility improvement
  • Arthritis management
  • Strength and conditioning
  • Post-surgery rehabilitation
Book for Geriatric Care

Our Treatment Methods

Manual Therapy

Hands-on techniques to manipulate joints and soft tissues to reduce pain, increase range of motion, and improve function.

Therapeutic Exercise

Customized exercise programs designed to improve strength, flexibility, and function specific to your condition.

Electrotherapy

Using electrical stimulation to reduce pain, improve circulation, and promote healing in injured tissues.

Balance Training

Specialized exercises to improve coordination, stability, and prevent falls, especially for older adults.
