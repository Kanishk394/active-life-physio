5 Effective Exercises to Relieve Lower Back Pain

Back Pain Exercise Therapy

Lower back pain is one of the most common complaints we hear at our clinic. Whether it's from sitting too long at a desk, poor posture, or an injury, back pain can significantly impact your quality of life. The good news is that specific exercises can help alleviate this pain and strengthen your back muscles to prevent future issues.

In this comprehensive guide, we'll explore five effective exercises that you can do at home to help relieve lower back pain. These exercises focus on strengthening your core muscles, improving flexibility, and promoting proper alignment.
